About Us

Exterior Experts You Can Trust

Exterior Property Maintenance specialises in diagnosing and repairing exterior issues that cause leaks and penetrating damp. Our expertise spans roofing, guttering, fascias, soffits, windows, doors, and full PVC roofline systems.

Based in Oldham, we proudly serve homeowners across Oldham and the surrounding areas, providing reliable solutions to protect and maintain properties.

We take pride in honest assessments, quality workmanship, and leaving every property better than we found it. No hard sell — just reliable results you can trust.
